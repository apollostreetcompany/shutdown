Here is a summary of the most critical findings:

**Market Size:**
- ~1.1-1.2 million businesses close in the US annually (BLS, Census Bureau)
- ~670,000-800,000 of those are LLCs or corporations requiring formal dissolution
- The total addressable market for dissolution-related services is approximately **$560M/year** (filing services only) to **$1.5B/year** (including related tax, legal, and compliance services)

**The Big Timing Opportunity:**
- The 2020-2021 pandemic boom produced ~10 million new business applications
- With a 49.4% five-year failure rate, roughly 5 million of those businesses will eventually need dissolution
- That closure wave is arriving **right now** (2025-2028)
- 2025 saw another 5.62 million new applications (+8.2% YoY), sustaining the pipeline

**The Gap in the Market:**
- ZenBusiness and Northwest Registered Agent (two of the top 5 formation companies) **do not offer dissolution services at all**
- The market splits between commodity filing ($99-$149 from MyCompanyWorks, LegalZoom, Bizee) and expensive startup-focused services ($2,500+ from SimpleClosure) or attorneys ($500-$5,000+)
- **Nobody owns the $200-$1,000 "guided dissolution" middle market**

**Recommended Strategy:**
- A $49-$79 state-by-state digital guide as an entry/lead-gen product
- A $199-$499 guided dissolution tier and a $999 complete dissolution package
- Content-led SEO strategy targeting "how to dissolve LLC in [state]" across all 50 states
- B2B channel opportunity with CPAs/accountants who handle dissolutions for clients
- Potential partnership with ZenBusiness or Northwest to fill their product gap

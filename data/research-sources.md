# Research Sources - State Business Dissolution Data

All data in this directory was researched from official state government sources in April 2026.

## Research Methodology
- 10 parallel research agents visited official Secretary of State (or equivalent) websites for all 50 states
- Each agent researched 5 states, visiting official .gov websites
- Data was cross-referenced against official fee schedules, form libraries, and statute databases
- 5 parallel data-building agents structured the research into JSON files
- A cross-reference pass corrected publication and tax clearance requirements
